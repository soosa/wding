Location

Tata 47.62,18.29, in Komárom-Esztergom county, is located some 70km (43miles) to the East from Budapest.

It is a charming historic town, also known as the town of lakes for its many waterfeatures located in its centre. The biggest lake, Old Lake, has a 7km (5mi) loop trail. The promenade leads completely around the lake. It is absolutely worth exploring, there are many sights to see.

Venue

Pikanto Restaurant

2890 Tata Tópart sétány 13. 47.64,18.33

This popular restaurant is right next to Old Lake in Tata. It has airconditioning and a generous terrace with a lake view. In the evening you can enjoy the setting of the sun with Tata's castle as a background.
